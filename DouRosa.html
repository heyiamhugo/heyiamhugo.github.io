DouRosa

Quinta de la Rosa is a pioneering Single Quinta winery in Portugal, known for its premium wines and ports. The estate also features a charming hotel and restaurant. Enter DouRosa, a new red wine from this esteemed brand. This red wine offers a gentler flavor profile, making it perfect for various occasions.

Its distinct rose hue further enhances its name and prestigious reputation. In their quest to elevate the packaging of their product, Quinta de la Rosa aimed to present a proposal that would embody their rich history, extensive market expertise, and the exceptional quality of their offerings. They sought an option that would distinguish them, shining a unique spotlight on their brand.

Drawing inspiration from the terraced vineyards of the Douro Wine region and the graceful movement of the wine itself, this proposal stands out among similar products in the market. The use of a vibrant pink color and captivating illustrations as the centerpiece gives it an unparalleled identity. To provide the client with a representation of the final product, a 3D model of the wine bottle was animated.
